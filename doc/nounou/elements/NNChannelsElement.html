trait NNChannelsElement extends NNElement

This trait of NNElement objects encapsulates the channel count information for electrophysiological and imaging data. Envisioned uses are for: - nounou.elements.data.NNData, - nounou.elements._layout.NNDataLayout. Channel names/count are intentionally mutable for nounou.elements.data.filters.NNDataFilter objects which conduct binning and therefore may change dynamically.
